Wth loss of Eden
NaNoGenMo MMXXI
https://github.com/Ben-Wormald/nanogenmo-2021

---

This transmission is our final act.
Our star is dark, our resources depleted; attempts to reach beyond our system have failed.
What follows is as much of our knowledge, culture, and art that we have the power to broadcast.
It is encoded in binary in our UTF-8 standard.
We hope you can translate our texts.
We hope what remains of our energy is enough to reach anyone else out there.
